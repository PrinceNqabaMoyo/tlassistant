Privacy statement

How Fundile approaches personal information and privacy.

This privacy statement explains, at a high level, how Fundile may collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you interact with the website, contact the team, create an account, or use Fundile services. It is informed by the principles of South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

Contact email info@fundile.com
Privacy focus POPIA-aligned handling of personal information
Applies to Website visitors, enquiries, account holders, and platform users

1. What information Fundile may collect

Depending on how you interact with Fundile, the platform may collect personal information such as your name, email address, school- or learning-related details, account information, communications you send to Fundile, subscription or payment-reference information, and technical information needed to operate the service.

2. Why that information may be used

Fundile may use personal information to provide access to the platform, respond to enquiries, support educational services, communicate about accounts or subscriptions, improve the service, maintain security, and comply with legal or operational obligations.

3. POPIA principles that guide the approach

Fundile aims to process personal information lawfully, reasonably, and in a way that respects privacy. This includes limiting collection to what is relevant, using information for defined purposes, protecting it with appropriate safeguards, and taking reasonable steps to keep information accurate and secure.

4. Sharing and disclosure

Fundile does not intend to share personal information unnecessarily. Information may be shared only where it is reasonably required to operate the platform, process services, support lawful administration, or comply with applicable legal obligations.

5. Security and retention

Fundile seeks to use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Personal information should only be retained for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law or legitimate operational needs.

6. Your rights and requests

Subject to applicable law, you may ask about the personal information Fundile holds about you, request corrections where information is inaccurate, and raise questions or concerns about privacy handling. Privacy requests and enquiries can be sent to info@fundile.com.

POPIA reference

This statement is informed by the principles of South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act. For a direct public reference, you can review the POPIA resource here: South African POPIA reference.

Important note

This page is a general privacy statement for website visitors and users. It should be reviewed periodically and, where necessary, updated as Fundile's services, data flows, and legal obligations evolve.
