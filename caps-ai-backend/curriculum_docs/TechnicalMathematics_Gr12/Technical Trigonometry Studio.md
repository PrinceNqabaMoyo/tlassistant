### Angles of elevation and depression

To solve problems involving angles of elevation and depression, we need to know the horizontal line (observer’s original eye level) and the non-horizontal line (observer’s lowered/raised eye level).



&nbsp;• If the endpoint is above the starting point, then we have an angle of elevation.

&nbsp;• If the endpoint is below the starting point, then we have an angle of depression.
