## ATTENTION

* Students are tested on their knowledge of the structure of the table.
* Students have to know the appropriate rows and their labels.
* In some cases, some row labels are left out and so the students have to put the appropriate row label and then input the appropriate figures and do any necessary calculations.
* In some cases some values are provided in the table with the answer book.
* When the row in an answer to an architype question  has '>', it means that the answer book did not include the row label, the student had to put it themselves or decide what the row is to be used for e.g totaling rows in the trade and other payable note  to the Statement of Financial Position
* When a monetary value is preceded with '\*', it means that the value was provided along with the answer book
* In some cases values such as those of 'cost of sales' in the Income statement may be provided but lacking the brackets that show that its outgoing funds. The student has to place the brackets themselves. When a bracketed figure is preceded by '(\*)', it means that the brackets were missing in the original architype question and students have to put the brackets themselves.
* For financial statements, rows are important. Therefore 'i' help button should be provided for each row.
