# Accounting for clubs and Non-profit organisations (NPOs)

## Curriculum Requirements

Definition and explanation of accounting concepts unique to nonprofit organisations (clubs):

• nonprofit organisations

• constitution

• accumulated fund

• membership fees

• entrance fees

• affiliation fees

• honorarium

• surplus

• deficit

Accounting cycle for non-profit organisations (clubs):

• preparation of Analysis Cash Book

• posting to the Ledger (particularly accounts unique to nonprofit organisations, viz. Accumulated Fund, membership fees, entrance fees, stock accounts, e.g. refreshments, ties, rugby jerseys)

• drafting of a Trial Balance

• preparation of Statement of Receipts and Payments

• understanding of differences between receipts \& income and;

payments \& expenses

## Notes

Non-profit organisations operate with the purpose of providing a service. The management committee of the business is responsible for managing the resources to the benefit of the organisation or club. Accounting concepts and principles are still applicable to the organisation or club.
