# Grade 10 Physical Sciences: Chemical Bonding

## Overview
Chemical bonding is the physical process responsible for the attractive interactions between atoms and molecules.

## Types of Bonds
1. **Covalent Bond**: Sharing of electrons between non-metal atoms.
   - Example: Water (H2O), Methane (CH4).
   - Render Visuals: Use the RDKit `render_visual` tool to output `type="chemistry"` and pass the SMILES string in `data`.
   - SMILES for Water: `O`
   - SMILES for Methane: `C`

2. **Ionic Bond**: Transfer of electrons from a metal to a non-metal.
   - Example: Sodium Chloride (NaCl).

3. **Metallic Bond**: Attraction between positive metal ions and the sea of delocalised electrons.

## Lewis Dot Structures
Lewis structures show the bonding between atoms of a molecule and the lone pairs of electrons that may exist in the molecule.
